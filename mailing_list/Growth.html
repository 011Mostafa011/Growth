The ability to set and reach your goals

Weather you would like to read more books, spend less time on netflix, engage and learn a new activity, with growth setting your screen time goals is as easy as defining them, you can have your breaks we make sure you do not BREAK your commitment "GET IT" for students and parents


For Parents Monitor Children Screen Time

In this day and age the school system has understood the importance of technology and with the teaching capabilities provided by tablets and smartphones it is no wonder that it has been incorporated into the school system. As promising as this sounds it means that grounding your children off their devices has become increasingly difficult. It is indeed true that their devices will be required fro school work, but how will parents be able to enforce how children use that screen time without removing access to the device With Growth simply, install the app into your children's device and set up a children account once completed you will be prompted to enter a generated code or scan a QR-code that will connect your device to your children's device . Voila you now set the schedule on how your children use their devices
